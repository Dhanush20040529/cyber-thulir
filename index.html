About Us

CyberThulir is a bootstrapped cybersecurity startup dedicated to democratizing cybersecurity education. We combine mentorship, hands-on labs, and a gamified Learning Management System (LMS) to create an engaging and impactful learning experience.

We go beyond the basics by developing cutting-edge tools and solutions across AI, Web3, IoT, and blockchain, tackling real-world security challenges. Our mission is to empower learners from all backgrounds—whether technical or non-technical—to build practical, job-ready skills in digital defense.

Our Vision

At CyberThulir, our vision is to create a future where cybersecurity is understandable, accessible, and achievable for everyone. We aim to empower individuals—regardless of their technical background—to build strong cyber skills through guided mentorship, practical learning, and innovative technologies.

By combining education with real-world application, we strive to solve today’s complex digital challenges and inspire the next generation of cyber defenders
